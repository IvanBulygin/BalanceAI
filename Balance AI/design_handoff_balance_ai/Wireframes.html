Wellness AI · wireframes

v0.1 — low-fi exploration
3 variations × 7 flows
mobile · 390×844

Original app concept inspired by the brief: a chat-first assistant that answers health questions by searching evidence-based supplement guides, returning ranked recommendations with dose, form, timing, and evidence strength. These are rough wireframes — structure over polish — so we can map the design space before committing to a direction.
